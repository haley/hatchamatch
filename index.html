HOW DOES IT WORK ?

If two people put each other as a priority, then so do we! When people put each other as top choice, they are guaranteed that match

We generate a series of random matchings, and evaluate their 'fitness' levels to get you a great matching!


After we've got a great matching, we email you, and you alone the final list of matches. Wouldn't want to ruin the big reveal!
